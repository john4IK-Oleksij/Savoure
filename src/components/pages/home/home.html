What’s on Your Mind?

We’ve answered the questions you didn’t know you had.

What are the restaurant's hours of operation?
Our restaurant is open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We strive to provide a welcoming atmosphere throughout the entire day, whether you're joining us for lunch or dinner. On weekends, the ambiance becomes even more lively, making it a perfect time to visit with friends or family. Please note that kitchen hours may vary slightly on holidays.
Do I need to make a reservation before coming?
Reservations are not required, but they are highly recommended during peak hours. Making a reservation ensures that you’ll have a table ready upon arrival, especially on weekends or special occasions. Walk-ins are always welcome, and we do our best to accommodate everyone. For larger groups, booking in advance is the best option.
Is there a vegetarian or vegan menu available?
Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes to suit different preferences. Our menu is thoughtfully crafted to include flavorful plant-based options that don’t compromise on taste. We also update our offerings seasonally to keep things fresh and exciting. If you have specific dietary requirements, our staff will be happy to assist.
Does this restaurant offer delivery services?
We provide delivery services through several popular platforms for your convenience. This allows you to enjoy our meals from the comfort of your home or office. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and demand. You can also place orders directly through our website for quick service.
Can I host a private event at the restaurant?
Yes, we welcome private events and special celebrations at our restaurant. Our team can help tailor the space and menu to suit your needs, whether it’s a birthday, corporate event, or intimate gathering. We offer flexible seating arrangements and personalized service. Contact us in advance to discuss availability and details.
Is parking available?
Parking is available near the restaurant, including both street parking and nearby parking lots. We aim to make your visit as convenient as possible, even during busy hours. Availability may vary depending on the time of day. We recommend arriving a bit earlier to secure a spot easily.
